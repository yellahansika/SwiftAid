Complete Emergency Toolkit

Explore the full capability of SwiftAid. Designed to be lightweight on your phone but heavy on protection.

Instant SOS Alerts

Trigger emergency notifications to nearby responders with one tap. SwiftAid automatically compiles your location, medical profile summary, and critical vitals to send a robust SOS packet to local EMS networks and your designated caregivers simultaneously.

  • One-swipe activation to prevent accidental false alarms.
  • Silent mode support for domestic danger scenarios.
  • Integration with regional 911/112 services.

Nearest Hospital Locator

Auto-detects your location and maps out the closest healthcare facilities. We sync with local health department databases to verify operational hours, ER capacity, and trauma level ratings before recommending a destination.

  • Real-time facility status (Open/Closed/Diverting).
  • Filter by specialty (Pediatric ER, Trauma, Cardiac).

Live Navigation Assistance

Step-by-step directions directly integrated into the app. Rather than switching to a map app, we provide a unified overlay that highlights the fastest routes while continuing to broadcast your location to dispatchers.

Multi-Language Support

Language barriers cost lives. SwiftAid supports 14 languages natively. The SOS dispatcher module automatically translates your text-to-speech inputs into the local operator's language when traveling abroad.

Offline Mode

When cellular networks fail, our Offline Mode ensures you maintain access to cached local hospital maps, comprehensive first aid protocols, and your pre-loaded emergency medical ID snapshot.

Accessibility Features

Engineered for inclusivity. SwiftAid allows voice command activations ("Hey Siri, Trigger SwiftAid"), features high-contrast visual modes for the visually impaired, and haptic feedback to confirm actions without looking.
